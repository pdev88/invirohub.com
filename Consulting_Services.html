Our Services

Invirotel prides itself on maintaining constant communication with its clients at an affordable rate. Insightful consulting services will assist the client to manage their relationship with the utility supplier as well as billing, tariff and energy managment.

Utility Management

Mediating between client and utility providers.
Communicating any changes of procedures and regulations.
Ensuring that the client receives bills on time.

Tariff Management

Assist with all aspects of tariff management.
Educate the client on available tariffs.
Ensure the client is on the best tariff structure to cut cost.
