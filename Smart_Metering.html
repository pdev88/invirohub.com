The concentrator is a central unit for remote reading, processing and controlling electricity and water meters that communicates with the meters over the low voltage power network, using two way PLC (Power Line Communication). The concentrator communicates with the control center over the cellular network GSM/GPRS. The Concentrator allows two-way communication access to all units/meters and can have up to 600 meters connected to one concentrator.
