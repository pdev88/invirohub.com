EMS INVIROTEL Energy Management (Pty) Ltd is
an integrated smart electricity, water metering and
propriety software company. Invirotel provides smart
metering, monitoring and evaluation tools for the
Public, Private as well as the Mass Residential
sectors.
INVIROTEL product range focuses on smart solutions
for the electricity and water sectors. INVIROTEL's
unique value-add is in the software harnessing the data
gathering and control abilities of the hardware devices.
The service requires software, hardware and installation
elements.
The Invirotel skills development approach is based
on internship programs with FET Colleges in
South Africa. Mentorship and training is offered to
selected unemployed graduates who join the
internship program with Invirotel.
