ABOUT US
INVIROHUB is an integrated energy saving business which was established four years ago in response to the growing need of smart energy solutions to provide positive changes for utilities, municipalities as well as to provide greener communities.
Invirohub technology is empowering remote and impoverished rural communities by providing them with modern, state-of-the-art technology and communication networks.
