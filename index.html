EMS INVIROTEL Energy Management (Pty) Ltd is
an integrated smart electricity and water metering
and propriety software company. Invirotel provides
smart metering, monitoring and evaluation tools for
the Public, Private as well as the Mass Residencial
sectors.
INVIROTEL product range focuses on smart solutions
for the electrical and electricity energy sector. The
service requires software, hardware and installation
elements. INVIROTEL's unique value-add is in the
software harnessing the data-gathering and control
abilities of the hardware devices.
INVIROTEL technology is empowering remote and
impoverished rural communities by providing them
with modern, state-of-the-art technology and
communication networks.
