Services

INVIROHUB is an integrated technology that incorporates smart meters for electricity, water and gas as well as proprietary software to provide comprehensive utility, energy and revenue management solutions for Utilities, Municipalities, Commercial Properties and Light-Industrial clients.
All INVIROHUB products are managed, maintained and controlled from a centralized digital hub accessible over any Internet connection 24/7.
Access is simple, secure, fast and mobile giving all clients and their clients real time information and control over :


Recoveries and Revenue Control : Supply side consumption from the utility is compared to actual consumption for the purpose of measuring over/under recoveries or incorrect metering or billing by the utility.Invirohub users can use the Invirohub data in disputes and recoveries from the utility.Customers in arrears can be remotely disconnected or their rating can be down-graded (for example: from a 100A rating to a 20A rating). Remote connection and disconnection fees can be levied. Revenue control measures can be used to collect other outstanding bills such as water and rent.
Vending & Settlement : End-to-end comprehensive vending or prepaid electricity from the Invirohub or mobile devices allows the Customer to buy electricity online. Meters are loaded in real-time after confirmation of the purchase no need for STS codes to key in after purchase. Conventional post-paid bills can also be settled on the Invirohub. Customers have detailed account information and transaction history. Property Owners have detailed payment information with alerts for delinquent payers.
Billing & Tarrifs : Invirohub has a unique, proprietary, patent-pending tariff engine catering for all types of tariffs spot block; standard time of use. Accurate comprehensive Utility bills are generated either on demand or according to pre-determined schedule based on the relevant tariffs. Bills can show hourly and daily usage for the premises or for specific appliances in the premises. Bills can be transmitted to customers in any electronic format. Customers and Property Owners have up-to-date on-the-go billing information.
Demand Control & Energy Management : Two-way communication with Invirohub devices enables Invirohub users to manage energy at installed sites: demand control; peak control; load control; timers; alerts; and remote cut offs and reduction of capacity. Energy management can be on demand or automated with preset configuration or in reaction to specific events or thresholds.
Metering : Utility Metering, Sub Metering, Check Metering, Appliance & Circuit Metering. Metering of Water, Gas and Electricity. Metering of the a range of data points: Watts; Amps; Voltage; Frequency; Power Factor; kwh; kva; kvar; maximum demand; green-house-gas emissions.
