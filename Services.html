INVIROHUB is an integrated technology that incorporates smart meters for electricity, water and gas as well as proprietary software to provide comprehensive utility, energy and revenue management solutions for Utilities, Municipalities, Commercial Properties and Light-Industrial clients. All INVIROHUB products are managed, maintained and controlled from a centralized digital hub accessible over any Internet connection 24/7. Access is simple, secure, fast and mobile giving all clients and their clients real time information and control over :
Metering: comprehensive metering of electricity, water and gas.  Utility Metering, Sub Metering, Check Metering, Appliance & Circuit Metering. Metering of a range of data points: Watts, Amps, Voltage, Power, Frequency, Power Factor, kwh, kva, kvar, and green-house-gas emissions.

Demand Control & Energy Management: Two-way communication with Invirohub devices enables Invirohub users to manage energy at installed sites: demand control, peak control, load control, timers, alerts, remote cut offs and reduction of capacity. Energy management can be on demand or automated with preset configuration or in reaction to specific events or thresholds.

Billing & Tariffs: Invirohub has a unique, proprietary, patent-pending tariff engine catering for all types of tariffs like spot, block, standard and time of use(TOU). Accurate and comprehensive Utility bills are generated either on demand or according to pre-determined schedule based on the relevant tariffs. Bills can show hourly and daily usage for the premises or for specific appliances in the premises. Bills can be transmitted to customers in any electronic format. Customers and Property Owners have up-to-date and on-the-go billing information.

Vending & Settlement: End-to-end comprehensive vending from the Invirohub platform through over the counter (cash), credit card, internet purchase (EFT), mobile purchase (wallet/ SMS) and purchases through Invirohub vending affiliates. Vending is available on the internet and on mobile phones allowing customers to purchase electricity easily and conveniently. Customers have detailed account information and transaction history. Property Owners have detailed payment information with alerts for delinquent payers.

Recoveries and Revenue Control: Supply side consumption from the utility is compared to actual consumption for the purpose of measuring over/under recoveries or incorrect metering or billing by the utility. Invirohub users can use the Invirohub data in disputes and recoveries from the utility. Customers in arrears can be remotely disconnected or their rating can be down-graded (for example: from a 100A rating to a 20A rating). Remote connection and disconnection fees can be levied. Revenue control measures can be used to collect other outstanding bills such as water and rent.
