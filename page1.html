INVIROHUB is an integrated smart meter and propriety Software Company which was established five years ago in response to the growing need of smart energy solutions. To provide positive to education, understanding and ultimately positive changes for utilities, municipalities as  well as greener
communities.

INVIROHUB’s services are available 24/7 online and mobile.We offer a number of smart metering, monitoring and evaluation solutions that will provide comprehensive utility, energy and revenue management solutions for utilities, Municipalities, Commercial Properties and Light-Industrial clients.
INVIROHUB technology is empowering remote and impoverished rural communities by providing them with modern, state-of-the-art technology and communication networks.
