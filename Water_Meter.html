RF Terminal Unit – RFTU


The RF communication Terminal Unit - RFTU is an unlicensed RF radio communication link from a remote wireless unit. It can be used to send Water data from any pulse water meter to the Power Line Communication (PLC) onto the INVIROHUB system.
WATER READING


The transmitted signals are received by any near-by INVIROHUB meter measuring electricity or any dedicated RF receiver.

The receiver introduces the data into the power line using Power Line Communication (PLC) to be received by the INVIROHUB
concentrator which stores the data for later report.

No additional wires are required for reading the Water meters.

No long distance radio communication infrastructure and licensing required.
