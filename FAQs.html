1.What is a smart meter?

A smart meter is an electrical meter that records and monitors electricity, water and gas in real time. Smart meter enables two way communication between meter and central control system. This communication enables monitoring and Automated Meter Reading (AMR) as well as Advance Meter Infrastructure (AMI) for utilities, municipalities, multi-tenanted buildings and residences.

2.How does a smart meter work?

Smart meters work in a variety of different ways, including using wireless mobile phone type technology to send data (GSM/GPRS). It communicates directly with your energy supplier and this will allow meter readings to be sent to your energy supplier remotely without a hassle.

2.1.How does Invirohub smart meter work?
Each meter communicates to a concentrator which is situated on the low voltage side of the transformer, using power lines communication (PLC).
•Invirohub Smart Meters can be installed inside or outside the customer's residence.
•The concentrator can communicate with up to 500 meters on the same transformer.
•The two-way communication enables reading, fraud and bypass detection, re-programming, connection and disconnection remotely.
•The concentrator communicates to a control center using the cellular network (GSM/GPRS).
•The control center can integrate into other systems of the distribution company, such as billing, vending and grid control.
•Each electricity meter can receive readings from a water and/or gas meter or both, using Radio Frequency.
•Loads in the customer’s residence can be remotely controlled using an Appliance Control Device (ACD) that communicates via PLC to the concentrator.

5.What is PLC?

Power Line Communication (PLC) is a critical and important energy control networking technology for smart meter. PLC enables sending and receiving of data over existing power cables.

6.What are AMI and AMR?

Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) is an old one way communication technology that only collects electricity, water and gas energy consumption and transfers the data from the meters on the property to an external device. Data is harvested either by walk-by, drive-by or fixed network but regardless of how the meter is read, the communication is one way.

7. Can I have smart meter if I am a credit or prepaid customer?

Yes, Invirohub smart meter work in credit and prepayment form. In fact prepaid customer may have more flexible payment options like remote top-up facilities.

8. Will smart meter save me money?

The Invirohub smart meter itself won’t save you money, but you will have an insight into how to lower your bills (energy saving). If you know where you are using energy you can then make informed decisions to save.
