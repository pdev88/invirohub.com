Operations

Deployment along this entire value chain is executed in partnership with associated companies, partners and other third parties. The core partners in the execution process are:



- a division of Bidvest - national distribution and market presence.
- ICT training and software development Locally based small, medium enterprises - augmentation of national      sales, deployment and installation.
