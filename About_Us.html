Our Services
EMS INVIROTEL Energy Management (Pty) Ltd is an integrated smart electricity and water metering and propriety software company. Invirotel
provides smart metering, monitoring and evaluation tools for the Public, Private as well as the Mass Residencial sectors. Invirotel is jointly owned by
VOLTEX (PTY) LTD which is the largest distributor of electrical equipment in South Africa and PDEV (Professional Development Institute) providing
enterprise software development. INVIROTEL was established for the setting of new standards in the green technology sector.
