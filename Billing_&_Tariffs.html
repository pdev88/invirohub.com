Billing & Tariffs
INVIROHUB owns patent pending algorithms that enables the support of any utility tariff.

The INVIROHUB tariff engine has templates for Straight Tariffs, Time of Use Tariffs (TOU),Block Tariffs and Spot Tariffs.

Tariffs are created with easy to use on-line tariffs.

The entire tariff, billing schedule, billing production and billing tools.

The INVIROHUB billing product can support manual meter readings where manual readings are uploaded to the INVIROHUB portal.
Where INVIROHUB approved meters are installed, revenue control is possible. Delinquent utility consumers can be cut off or their supply can partially throttled or gradually cut off.
