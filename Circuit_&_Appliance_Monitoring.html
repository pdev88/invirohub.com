Our Services
INVIROHUB owns proprietary technology for monitoring each and every circuit in a building. The hardware comprises a Multi Clamper -a proprietary set of 8 or 12 Multi-Clampers which clip onto the circuit to be measured. Measurements can be made every 5 seconds of power, current consumption, power factor, reactive energy, carbon emissions, frequency and voltage.
