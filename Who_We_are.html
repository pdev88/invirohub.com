Ayal Rosenberg- Invirotel MD & Board / PDEV CTO

Ayal is the Chief Technology Officer of INVIROTEL and provides broader support to the Maredi Telecoms Group of
Companies. He was a Microsoft MVP (Most Valuabe Programmer) for Architecture and a previous Regional Director for
Microsoft SA. He was part of the Microsoft Patterns & Practices Smart Client and CAB Forums based in Redmond. Ayal
has been involved in the design, architecture and deployment of large-scale enterprise systems for the largest blu-chip
companies in South Africa. In the past five years Ayal has been involved in combining software with green initiatives and
smart energy.
INVIROTEL has a permanent staff compliment of fifteen, including a software and support team, a qualified lawyer, electrician and electronics
engineer. The leadership consists of the board of directors, including non-executive directors and the executive team. The INVIROTEL staff
compliment is supported by the Voltex sales force, installation and field teams.
Stanley Green - Voltex MD / Invirotel Chairman

Stan is a non-executive Chairman of the company and Managing Director of Voltex. Stan has been with Voltex for over 21
years, holding various roles including Chief Legal Officer. Stan holds a BA LLB qualification from Wits University.

Eric Immerman- Financial Director Voltex / Invirotel Board

Eric is a chartered accountant who works at Voltex as Finance Director, and provides financial administration support to
Invirotel. Eric has over 31 years of experience in senior financial management positions.
Lance Manala- Maredi CEO / Invirotel Board

Lance is the Chief Executive Officer of the company and chairman of Maredi Telecoms Group of Companies. Lance is the
founder of Maredi Telecoms and has held various senior executive and non-executive positions in different companies.
Lance is entrepreneurial, hands-on and passionate about genuine economic transformation. He holds a NHD (Chemical
Engineering from Cape Peninsula University of Technology), a Post Graduate Diploma ( Industrial Administration from the
University of Cape Town) and has attended the Board Leadership Programme (Gordon’s Institute of Business Science).
EMS INVIROTEL Energy Management (Pty) Ltd is an integrated smart electricity and water metering and propriety software company.
Invirotel provides smart metering, monitoring and evaluation tools for the Public, Private as well as the Mass Residencial sectors. Invirotel is
jointly owned by VOLTEX (PTY) LTD which is the largest distributor of electrical equipment in South Africa and PDEV (Professional
Development Institute) providing enterprise software development. INVIROTEL was established for the setting of new standards in the green
technology sector.
